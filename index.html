Story of

Erna & Reza

  • 2017

They first met at a PPI (Perhimpunan Pelajar Indonesia) event held at Erna’s campus, where Reza showed up wearing a Star Wars T-shirt. The shirt caught Erna’s attention, prompting her to take a photo of him—though her intention was to capture the T-shirt. Coincidentally, Reza also noticed her and was intrigued from the very first glance.

Not long after, they decided to follow each other on Instagram and started talking there.

  • 2018

Reza made the first move by inviting Erna to a concert in Kuala Lumpur featuring an Indonesian artist they both loved — marking their first (unofficial) date. Something special sparked between them that night, leading to more moments shared and memories made together.

Later that year, they decided to make their relationship official.

  • 2019 – 2020

Fresh out of university, life briefly took them on separate paths — Reza returned to Jakarta, while Erna remained in Kuala Lumpur to complete her internship. A few months later, they were joyfully reunited in Jakarta after Erna’s internship ended. But just as they were finding their rhythm again, the world came to a halt — COVID struck, and they found themselves separated once more by lockdowns.

  • 2021 - 2024

As lockdowns gradually eased, they were finally able to spend more time together in person. But in 2021, Erna received an exciting opportunity to work in Malaysia, and once again, they found themselves in a long-distance relationship — this time for three years. Despite the distance, their bond only grew stronger, until they were finally reunited in 2024 when Reza received a job offer in Malaysia.

  • 2025

After months of living in the same country and finally enjoying simple joys like weekend dates together, they felt it was time to take the next step. In January this year, with full hearts and a shared vision for the future, they made the decision to be more serious about their relationship. They called their parents to share the joyful news.

They were ready to get married.
